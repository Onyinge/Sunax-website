Company Background & History

SUNAX COMPANY (K) LTD was founded in 2021 as a construction firm based in Migori, Kenya. The company was officially registered on 28th October 2021 with registration certificate no. PVT-DLULXJ68 under the Companies Act, 2015 as a Private Limited Company.

Starting with a share capital of KES 10,000 divided into 20 ordinary shares of KES 500 each, the company has grown to become a reputable construction service provider in the region. Within a short period, we have established ourselves as a reliable partner for both public and private sector construction projects.

Our name "SUNAX" represents our commitment to the Suna region of Migori County while maintaining excellence in construction. We operate from our head office located on KSIII-SIRARI ROAD, adjacent to DTB Bank in Migori Town.

Our Vision

To be a leading construction works provider company of choice in Kenya and beyond, recognized for excellence, reliability, and innovation in construction services. We aim to expand our operations nationally while maintaining our commitment to quality and client satisfaction.

Our Mission

To provide quality, effective and efficient construction solutions to our clientele through proper project management, skilled personnel, and adherence to industry standards. We are committed to timely delivery, safety, and sustainable construction practices.
